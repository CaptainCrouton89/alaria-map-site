# Celestia return

Invariant: Celestia is the Material Plane's mirror flip-side, reached sideways across any edge. Return from Celestia requires that a soul or shadow anchor was preserved on the material side. The God War destroyed most anchors, trapping those who had crossed without one.

The apparent contradiction — Celestia return is impossible, yet the God War was a mass return from Celestia — resolves as follows.

Golden-Age migrants who crossed into Celestia while still alive could preserve their soul and shadow on the material side via anchor-vessels or kept bodies. That material anchor made return possible. The God War destroyed most of those anchors, trapping everyone whose anchor was gone. The contradiction was never real; the rule holds because the condition was never met by the people who are now trapped.

New souls who enter Celestia after death have no material anchor and cannot return.
