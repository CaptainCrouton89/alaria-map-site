# Calendar

Invariant: F/B is the absolute astronomical clock (the Great Cycle, tied to Aurus/Nydus drift and lunar alignments; observer-independent). SD/BSD is civil reckoning. Seventh Dawn is the current era. Now = 3376 SD. World Fire (10 BSD) ≈ year zero. Any SD date converts to F/B via the F/B position of the Seventh Dawn's start.

Two systems run simultaneously.

F/B is the absolute astronomical clock, the Great Cycle (0 → 25,300 → 0). It tracks Aurus/Nydus drift and lunar alignments. It is observer-independent and runs without interruption across eras.

SD/BSD is civil reckoning. Seventh Dawn is the current era. Now is 3376 SD. The World Fire at 10 BSD is the rough year zero of recorded history. Any SD date converts to F/B by knowing where in the Great Cycle the Seventh Dawn began.
