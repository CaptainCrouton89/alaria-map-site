# Magic system

Invariant: Four sources: Deoric (Azus; costs life), Psywinds (Ezz's thought/intention aspect), Faesong (Melera's emotion/harmony aspect of Ezz), Kethic (the caster's own emotion structured through the Elemental Planes via leylines). Psywinds and Faesong are two faces of the same Ezz — thought vs emotion — with no scholarly ambiguity.

Four sources, all fully settled.

Deoric is pure Azus — structure and command, costing life. Titans wield it natively. Daemons project it across the boundary between planes. Handmages use it through runes inscribed in titan blood. Ritualists access it via blood sacrifice.

Psywinds are the thought and intention aspect of Ezz. Psy magic, telepathy, prayer-transmission, and true-name propagation all flow through Psywinds. Thought only — not emotion. Emotion is Faesong's lane.

Faesong is Melera's own ambient emotion and harmony, the other face of Ezz. It is heard and felt, not commanded. Druids, fae, and bards work with it.

Kethic is distinct from Faesong. Where Faesong is Melera's emotion, Kethic is the caster's own emotion, structured through the Elemental Planes via leylines. Gaea's bridge-language.

Psywinds and Faesong are two faces of one Ezz — thought versus emotion — with no ambiguity or scholarly disagreement.
