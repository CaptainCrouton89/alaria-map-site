# First death

Invariant: Three strands: soul → Astral (cycles through Aurus), shadow → Malstaris (cycles through Nydus), spirit → Celestia (lasts only while the true name is remembered; forgotten means it ends permanently).

Souls and shadows predate Ezz. When Lyzaria died, she defined the original two-strand death — she carved the channels that every subsequent death flows through. The first channel is the Dreaming of Lyzaria (soul → Astral). The second is the First Dark (shadow → Malstaris). These exist because Lyzaria existed, not because the world made them.

The Ezz Rift, 11.5 million years later, threaded the third strand into that existing framework: spirit → Celestia, lasting as long as a true name is held in memory.

Lyzaria is the architect of mortality. Her own death was not incomplete and she is not a spiritless remnant. The absence of a spirit-strand in her death is simply a consequence of Ezz not yet existing.
