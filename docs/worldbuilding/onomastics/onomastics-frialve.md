# Frialve Onomastics

> **sparse sample — extrapolate conservatively**

**Author anchor:** Consonant-cluster openers (fr/sh/by), z-interior, short vowel terminals — sharp, canopy-carrying  
**Source pool (frozen — do not copy verbatim):** Frialve (race name), Bygos Shemazari (polity — treated as two-word compound)

## Sound palette
- **Frialve**: Fr + ial + ve — fr- cluster opener, -ial- complex vowel interior, -ve terminal
- **Bygos**: By + gos — by- opener (b+y blend), hard g interior, -os terminal
- **Shemazari**: She + ma + za + ri — Sh- opener, -ema- interior, z medial, -ri terminal
- Consonant palette: cluster openers (Fr-, Sh-, By-); z and g as interior consonants; terminals are short and closed (-ve, -os, -ri, -ari)
- Vowel behavior: interior vowel sequences (ial, ema) permitted; terminals are clipped; no long open-vowel close
- The z-sound appears in both words of the city name (Shemazari) and rhymes with the v-sound in Frialve — z/v are the register's interior identity consonants
- Forbidden / off-style: long liquid openers; pure-vowel names; anything soft and flowing; the bird-communication culture means names project well — soft names would be lost in a forest canopy

## Morphemes & affixes
- Cluster openers: **Fr-** (race name), **Sh-** (Shemazari), **By-** (Bygos) — no single opener is universal; all three are valid
- Interior z or v: the consistent phonemic signal across all three words (-ial-ve in Frialve; z in Shemazari)
- Terminals: **-ve** (Frialve), **-os** (Bygos), **-ri/-ari** (Shemazari) — short-closed terminals only
- Two-word place names: Bygos Shemazari = settlement-type + locale-qualifier; single-word personal names

## Naming customs
- Bird-call communication shapes naming: fr-/sh-/by- cluster openers carry across forest distances; soft openers dissipate
- Settlement names are typically two-word compounds in the Bygos Shemazari pattern
- Personal names are single-word, cluster-opened, z- or v-interior
- Z-sound is the strongest shared frialve phonemic marker — it appears in both halves of the polity name

## Worked examples (derived, not copied)
- **Frizael** — personal: Fri- (cf. Frialve's Fr-) + -zael (z + ael, -ial-variant with z interior replacing v); a given name
- **Bygori** — settlement name: Byg- (cf. Bygos' By-g) + -ori (-ori terminal, cf. -ri in Shemazari prefixed by o); a canopy-district name
- **Shevzar** — rank or name: Shev- (Sh- + ev interior, cf. Shemazari's She-) + -zar (z+ar, Shemazari's -zari contracted to hard close); a hunter-caller title or personal name
