The empire's lifeblood—a narrow passage connecting the Middle Sea to the open ocean beyond. This chokepoint means all maritime trade between the Middle Sea kingdoms and the wider world must pass through Tarkhon-controlled waters.

Tarkhetan, the capital, sits directly on the strait. Its massive navy and fire-mage garrison ensure that every ship pays tribute. Those who refuse are burned.
