### Overview
Hunched, fanged creatures resembling gray hags, with tattered wings that can't carry them off the ground. Their mere presence makes you fall asleep, the way a sandman's would, and the pull is immensely hard to resist — though you stay fully aware that the creature wants to kill you the whole time you are going under.
