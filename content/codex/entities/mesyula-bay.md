The western coastline indents around Mesyula Bay, a shallow body of water rich in fish and seabirds. The Korel occasionally fish these waters from small hide boats, but they build no permanent fishing villages. Foreign ships sometimes anchor in the bay to take on fresh water, though doing so risks attracting Korel attention.
