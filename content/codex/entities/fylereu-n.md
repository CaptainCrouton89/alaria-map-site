A secondary peak in the Mount Tolaria range, once home to an astronomical observatory operated by the mage-kings. The observatory was studying the relationship between celestial movements and magical flows when the Apparatus activated. The building still stands, its instruments still aligned, but the astronomers are long dead and their notes are written in ciphers no one has cracked.
