A moderate-sized mountain range runs diagonally from the southwest to the northeast, roughly bisecting the continent. From south to north, it comprises:

- **Blood Mountains** (south): Home to an underground realm of vampiric gnomes
- **Mez**: Mountain state
- **Dern**: Mountain state
- **Thorum** (north): Northernmost mountain state
