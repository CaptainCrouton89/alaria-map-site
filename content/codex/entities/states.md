Three nations control the Crimson Coast, each shaped by the region's bloody history:

#### Tangiern

The largest state by territory, Tangiern is a human kingdom occupying the western two-thirds of the region. Its people—hardy foresters and hunters—have carved settlements from the vast Fragenstor Mielthøn over generations. Capital: **Nøsen**.

*See: Tangiern*

#### Klevnaf

A Winter Elf kingdom controlling the central coastal strip. Klevnaf claims legitimacy through Prince Taoinor, uncle to the murdered King Istor XXVI. They hold Murder Creek and the cursed ground where ancient King Ghy fell. Capital: **Svedlind**.

*See: Klevnaf*

#### Istora

The eastern Winter Elf kingdom, ruled by Queen Lamenrae—daughter of the murdered Istor XXVI. The Istori consider Klevnaf traitors and usurpers. Their realm follows the Sova Chyda river from the Suftos Hills to the sea. Capital: **Tuur**.

*See: Istora*
