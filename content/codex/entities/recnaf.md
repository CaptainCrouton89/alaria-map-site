On the southern shore of the eastern peninsula, Recnaf is Roule's quietest city—a sheltered harbor town more focused on fishing than politics. The city lacks Fozyader's grandeur, Teldib's military importance, or Kabilcuz's historical pretensions. Locals consider this a virtue.

Recnaf's fishing fleet is the largest in Roule, and the city's markets supply much of the nation's seafood. The work is hard, the people are practical, and outsiders who put on airs are quietly mocked. There's a stubbornness here that the other cities lack—Recnaf was settled by colonists who wanted distance from halfling high society, and that independence persists.

The city maintains informal trade relationships with communities the Honey Lords would prefer not to acknowledge. Fishing boats sometimes return with cargo that didn't come from the sea. The city watch asks few questions. Recnaf takes care of its own.
