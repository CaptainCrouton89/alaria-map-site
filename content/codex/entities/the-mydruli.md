The largest river in eastern Wycendeula, originating in the central Kilbyurn Mountains. The Mydruli flows east-southeast across the Plains of Wycendeula, eventually reaching the coast south of the Cobbled Coast peninsula.

The Mydruli is fed by glacial melt and runs cold year-round. Its waters are clean and support abundant fish populations—one of the few reliable food sources on the plains besides the buffalo herds. Satyr camps cluster along its banks more densely than elsewhere, though even here settlement is temporary.

Ancient bridge foundations span the Mydruli at several points, remnants of the human civilization that once occupied these plains. The satyr ignore them, but treasure hunters occasionally attempt to excavate the riverbanks.
