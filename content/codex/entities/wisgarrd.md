A dwarven city-state inland from Maun, built into the foothills where the Pearly Mountains descend toward the coast. Wisgarrd is home to the School of Jewelry, where master craftsmen teach the art of gem-cutting and, to select students, the secrets of magical enchantment. See the full state entry for details on its curriculum, the legendary Book of Gems, and its complicated relationship with Maun.

The Peonie River flows past Wisgarrd's walls, its banks thick with the flowering plants that give the river its name. Jewelers draw inspiration from the blooms; some say no Wisgarrd piece is complete without at least one element borrowed from the peonie's form.
