South of Levke, Andvik guards the approaches from Nashua. This fortress-city has seen more combat than any other Strømgodden settlement, its walls scarred by generations of skirmishes with Ix'Vasyla raiders.

Andvik's warriors specialize in anti-lizardfolk tactics—fighting in the mountain passes where the cold-blooded Ix'Vasyla are at disadvantage, striking at dawn when the lizardfolk are sluggish, and maintaining the vigilance that has kept Nashua's forces from ever reaching Levke.

The city's **Venom-Scarred** regiment consists entirely of warriors who have survived Ix'Vasyla bites—a badge of honor that marks them as tested against Trømgar's most persistent enemy.
