Sacred springs bubble up in Ierya's heart—cold, clear water emerging from deep beneath the Walking Forest. The springs have subtle magical properties: wounds heal faster for those who bathe in them, poisons are neutralized, and those who drink from the source sometimes experience prophetic dreams.

The water comes from deep underground, filtered through root-networks that span miles. It carries something of the Grayls' slow vitality—a connection to their dreaming awareness. The Grayls are protective of the springs. This is the one place in the Walking Forest where they actively respond to threats.
