The capital, located in the central plains. Site of the great temple where the Kazigh rules, attended by servants selected through horse ritual and killed at age 30. The spiritual and political heart of Ta Minn.

For generations the temple was where each season's riders of the Ride received the Kazigh's blessing before they went out. The blessing is still given. It now sends cavalry north to the Kanüli Daha Plains to hold the frontier rather than out across open grass to widen it.
