The **Honor River** rises in the eastern highlands and flows westward through Avalon's heart, eventually emptying into Munari Inlet. Its valley contains the kingdom's best farmland and most of its population.

**Honor Creek** branches northeast from the main river, draining the Pathanul region. The creek's waters are said to reveal glimpses of the future to those who drink from certain pools—though the priests warn that unguided visions bring madness more often than wisdom.

**Honor Moss** is the marshy region where the river slows near Tarolin, spreading into wetlands thick with reeds and waterfowl. The Moss produces medicinal herbs used in time-magic rituals.

**Pale Run** flows through the southern forests, eventually joining the river systems that drain toward Chimea.
