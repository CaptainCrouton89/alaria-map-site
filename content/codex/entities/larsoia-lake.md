A larger lake in the central Grognil, partially fed by underground springs that keep sections from freezing completely. The water is drinkable and the shores occasionally show signs of old camps, but no permanent settlement has ever taken hold. Travelers who stay more than a few nights report increasingly vivid dreams of drowning—not in water, but in absolute stillness, unable to move or cry out.
