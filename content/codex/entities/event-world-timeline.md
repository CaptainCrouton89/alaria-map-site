Time has consisted of three eons of full world cycles. Each cycle ends with a reality-warping event. The current cycle is still relatively young.

### The Three Eons

1. **The Early Times** — the first eon; Azus and Melera shape the cosmos; the Age of Titans rises and falls; 4 billion–23.5 million years ago
2. **The Gaeaic Eon** — the second eon; Ezz floods Alaria; Gaea is born; 12 million–~10,209 years ago
3. **The Modern Era** — the third and current eon; from Hykravones the Shattering to present day (~10,209 years ago–Now)
