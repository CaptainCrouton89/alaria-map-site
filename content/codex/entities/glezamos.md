The current capital of Zelidia sprawls across both banks of the Sovia, connected by bridges woven from living vines. Glezamos was built hastily after the fall of Iirmos, and its architecture still carries that desperate, improvised quality—structures that grew organically around the needs of refugees rather than following any grand design.

The Temple of the Hungry Canopy dominates the city's center, where the High Priestesses conduct their sacrifices and commune with the jungle-spirits. Foreign delegations (the rare few permitted to live) are received in the Bleeding Court, an open plaza where the stones are permanently stained rust-red.
