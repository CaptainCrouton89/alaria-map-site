Enapay is the ancient Dwelyn monarchy occupying the Green Wilds at the edge of the Ishnit Jungles. Its queens are bonded to the emerald dragon Surrey Mahaila through a ritual known only to the royal line. The capital, Lise, sits at the Ishtar River delta where the Green Wilds thin enough to build. Queen Teyara Greenmantle holds court there, directing both clan mediation and the ongoing war effort.

Enapay claims the entirety of the Green Wilds, though effective control extends only as far as Dwelyn settlements and patrol routes. The nation's eastern territories—the Green Marches—remain contested with East Naruaghin tribes. The nation has no coastline; the Gulf of Norag lies south of Lise but Naruaghin territory and cursed waters prevent direct access. All trade flows overland or upriver along the Ishtar.
