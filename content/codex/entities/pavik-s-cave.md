A coastal cave system on the western shore, near the Gulf of Kazül. The lower galleries flood at high tide, and the fishing villages above them keep a standing rule that no one goes below the tide-line after dark. They say something lives down there. The catch off this stretch of coast is poor, and the villagers do not think that an accident.

<!-- author-notes -->
GM-true (player-facing prose keeps it a coastal legend): a large solitary marine predator dens in the flooded lower chambers and ranges the gulf to feed, which is why the local catch is thin. Mundane in origin, not Ederhi and not tied to the Glassing — Pavik's Cave is on the western coast, away from the Jahazai works. The legend has a concrete answer (closing the prior open-ended "something living in the depths" per the no-lazy-mystery rule); a GM can scale the creature to the party.
