A northern mountain town positioned at a key pass through the Drystone Mountains. Jamtaland serves as Breia's gateway to the northern desert, and most trade entering or leaving the state passes through its markets. The town is fortified, not against armies but against the rocs and other flying predators that hunt the mountain passes.
