Istora's sacred forest, a dense evergreen woodland along the Sova Chyda's middle reaches. Unlike Whitewood's pale trees, Winterwood is dark and thick—a forest of shadows even at midday. The Istori believe their ancestors' spirits dwell here between incarnations. Queen Lamenrae's coronation took place within Winterwood, defying tradition that demanded the ceremony occur in the now-Klevnaf-controlled ancestral capital.
