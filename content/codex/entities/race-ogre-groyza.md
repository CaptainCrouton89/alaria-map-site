The Groyza are a people of the ogre, not a separate bloodline — swamp ambushers and herbalists whose way of life is shaped by the bog, not by a biological split from ogre stock.

The Groyza are swamp-dwelling ogres who have made the murky waters and dense vegetation of the southern marshlands their home. A Groyza who has lived in the bog wears its colors, skin stained a greenish-brown mottle by peat water and the crushed marsh-plants they daub on for concealment, until they all but disappear against the reeds. They run leaner than their plains cousins as well, worn down by wading labor and a thin wetland diet, though still massive by any other race's standards.

The Groyza are master ambush predators, using the swamp's natural concealment to surprise prey and enemies alike. They are also skilled herbalists, brewing potent poisons and medicines from the unique flora of their wetland homes. Other races approach Groyza territory with extreme caution, for the swamps are full of hidden dangers—and the ogres are often the least of them.
