Vraxuni was an orc naturalist of the Glivornaxi tribal territories who spent her adult life cataloguing the creature ecology of the Plenjorn Swamp and adjacent borderlands. Her field notes — compiled posthumously by her apprentices — are the foundational creature-taxonomy for the swamp region, and the only source that distinguishes between the region's dozen shadow-adjacent species by behavior rather than appearance.
