Blossom Valley opens along Apketrii's southern slopes, a broad lowland sheltered from sea wind by the Diamondback Mountains to the north. The valley floor supports the island's most productive growing land—tended by families who hold hereditary grazing rights along the river flats. Spring flooding enriches the soil; the valley earns its name from the mass flowering that follows.

Keletus depends on Blossom Valley to feed the populations its naval dockyards cannot. The produce trade from valley to port towns is one of the few fully domestic supply chains Keletus controls without outside interference.
