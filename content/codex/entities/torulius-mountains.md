The northern range, less dramatic than the Sunwing Peaks but more accessible. Mining operations extract iron and copper from the Torulius slopes, feeding Grustos's modest metalworking industry.

The mountains are not uninhabited—small communities of herders and miners dot the lower slopes—but they lack the dramatic populations of the Sunwing Peaks. No sun elves, no blood-goblins, no giants. Just rock, ore, and people trying to make a living.
