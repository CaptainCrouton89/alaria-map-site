A southern city along the Mhunta River, the agricultural heart of loyalist Poum Titch. Grain barges from Bobeitio's irrigation networks feed Lekia and Tadinu both. The fields here are ancient, worked by the same Scalawag families for generations.

Since the war began, Bobeitio has become a loyalist stronghold and the primary supply depot for southern forces. Refugee camps have doubled the city's population. Food that once sold in markets now goes directly to army quartermasters. The farmers grumble, but they fear the northerners more than they resent their own government.
