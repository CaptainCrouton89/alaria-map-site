The smallest of the three craters in The Impact Craters cluster, Nisk is dry—its bowl filled with frost-shattered stone rather than water. Whatever fell here struck differently from its siblings. The crater floor is scattered with fragments of a dark metalite matching no known ore, and some of these pieces remain warm to the touch regardless of how long they sit in the cold.

The Neth say Nisk is where a star fell and died. They leave offerings at the crater's rim—small carvings, braided grass, tokens of remembrance—offering little explanation of who or what they honor. Traders who have recovered the warm fragments sell them south, where artificers pay well for even small pieces.
