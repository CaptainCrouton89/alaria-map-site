A massive volcanic mountain rising from the center of the Sandreach desert—a dramatic landmark visible for miles across the barren landscape. This volcano was the site where the legendary Demon Horn of Fire was forged, drawing on the convergence of fire and light leylines beneath the mountain. That same convergence once fed the Temple of Bryn, a desert sanctuary raised over the meeting of the two seams; when Belu Jenari pushed up burning out of the sand, the fire side surged and the temple was scoured under with it.
