A stretch of rocky coastline and sheltered bays along the Seacleft Coast, home to the most skilled fishermen in Alaria. Enavadi is less a unified nation than a confederation of fishing villages bound by shared customs, intermarriage, and a deep suspicion of outsiders. The land is poor—thin soil, few resources, no strategic value—but the waters are rich, and the Enavadi have built everything around them.

The coast runs roughly north-south, with Edko Sound and the Floss Islands forming a natural harbor complex in the north, and Prince's Bay opening to the south toward the waters around Gyosha. Between them lie a handful of fishing villages, each with its own specialty and character, collectively producing catches that feed markets across the Seacleft Coast.
