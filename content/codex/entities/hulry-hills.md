The Hulry Hills are rolling highland country in the northwestern Crimson Coast, between Hilda's Forest and the foothills of the Dragon's Spine. This is grazing land, sparsely settled, worked by sheep and goat herders who keep their flocks moving and their eyes on the passes. The hills are the first ground to see anything come down out of the mountains, and the herders are reckoned to owe the lowlands fair warning when it does. The streams that gather here feed the River of Wights as it starts its run south, and the herders water their flocks well above where it turns, never below. Above that line the water is only cold. Below it, the hill families say, it stops being water you trust.
