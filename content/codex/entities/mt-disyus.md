The northern mountain, rising to heights where human visitors struggle to breathe. The thin air suits the Rhea perfectly—their air-elemental nature finds the altitude comfortable, even preferable.

The mountain's upper reaches are carved with monastery complexes, meditation halls, and the ancient libraries of **Eynmore**. Lower slopes support terraced gardens where Rhea cultivate rare alpine herbs.

**Features:**
- **Eynmore** (capital): Monastery city of the Rhea, population ~8,000
- **The High Halls**: Meditation chambers open to the sky
- **The Eternal Library**: Knowledge collected over a thousand years
- **The Garden Terraces**: Rare herbs for healing and magical work
