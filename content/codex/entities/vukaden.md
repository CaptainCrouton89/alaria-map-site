The northernmost village, closest to Edkovic and practically its suburb. Many Vukaden residents work in the capital but prefer village life—quieter, cleaner, with better neighbors. The village is known for **boat-building**. The Vukaden hull design—shallow draft, stable in chop, easily rowed when winds fail—has become the standard fishing vessel across the Seacleft Coast. Master shipwrights here are as respected as master fishermen, and apprenticeships are fiercely competitive.
