The capital of Vystrilik, situated in the central-western jungle at the base of the Tambrielle Mountains. Vystril is a prosperous trading city controlling the primary mountain passes. The city's architecture reflects its role as a crossroads—influences from the coast, the jungle interior, and the eastern seaboard blend in its buildings and marketplaces.

The city maintains extensive warehouses where goods are sorted, taxed, and transferred between jungle canoes and the pack trains that cross the mountains.
