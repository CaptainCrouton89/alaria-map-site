On the eastern slopes of the Tambrielle Mountains lies the Shambling Forest, a dense woodland with an unsettling reputation. The forest earned its name from travelers' reports of trees that seem to move when unobserved—paths shift, landmarks relocate, and those who enter often emerge miles from where they expected. Whether this is natural disorientation, magical influence, or something stranger remains disputed.
