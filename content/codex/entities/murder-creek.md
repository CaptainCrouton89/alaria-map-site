The most infamous waterway in the Crimson Coast. This river runs through the heart of Klevnaf, its waters permanently darkened by an ancient curse. King Ghy the Elder—the last ruler of the unified Winter Elves—was murdered on its banks over a thousand years ago. The creek remembers. Those who drink from it experience vivid hallucinations of the assassination, seeing it from different perspectives each time. The elves consider the water sacred and profane simultaneously.
