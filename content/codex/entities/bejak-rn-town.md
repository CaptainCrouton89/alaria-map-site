Population: ~600

A highland town in the eastern reaches, positioned where the Sova Chyda descends from its mountain sources. Bejakørn serves as Istora's gateway to the Dragon's Spine Coast—limited trade still flows through here, and the town's inns host travelers heading east or west.

The town also houses Istora's armories and training grounds, positioned far from the front lines where they can operate without interruption. Young elves from across the kingdom come here to learn the warrior's arts before deployment to the Suftos line.
