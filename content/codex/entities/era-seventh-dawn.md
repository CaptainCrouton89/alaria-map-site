The Seventh Dawn is the current age of Alaria, beginning at 0 SD (after the World Fire) and extending to the present (3,376 SD). It is the third eon's most recent named period. The Dark Ages mark its opening chapter; the modern political world of 3,376 SD is its present state.

### Notable Centuries

- **300 SD** — The Time Puzzle (312 SD)
- **400 SD** — Hive elves at their peak power
- **1700 SD** — The Sivakr are driven underground
- **1800 SD** — Tiira ascends to godhood (god of the silver elves)
- **2900 SD** — Chimea crosses the Pelakan Sea, invading Gyendoveir and Yystrilik; a bloody war continues to this day
- **3376 SD** — The present day
