A lakeside town on Sopona Strempele, the accessible lake. Fishing, swimming, and a relaxed atmosphere draw visitors from across Grustos—the closest thing the state has to a resort town. The proximity to Enuau Kemjallb adds exotic appeal, even though the sun elf city is neither visible nor accessible from here.
