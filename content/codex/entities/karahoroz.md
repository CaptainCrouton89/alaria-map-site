Avalon's eastern city, Karahoroz sits where the Honor River approaches Dern. It serves as administrative center for the eastern fiefdoms and as a trading hub with the mountain states. The city's priests maintain close ties with Dern—the mountain kingdom has its own traditions, but respects Avalon's theocracy enough to cooperate on border matters.
