The central forest, vast and ancient. Named for the distinctive spine-like ridges on the fir trees that dominate the region. Spinefir is the heart of what remains of Gyv civilization—far enough from the war to be safe, connected enough by river to matter.

Theochori sits on Spinefir's southern edge, where the Skleidyon River emerges from the trees. The forest provides the capital's security: any army marching on Theochori would have to pass through miles of ambush-ready woodland first.
