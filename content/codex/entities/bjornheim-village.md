Population: ~200

A frontier village in the southern reaches, positioned to watch the wild country between Istora and the Dragon's Spine Coast. Bjornheim's residents are hardened—the village has been raided by bandits, monsters from the mountains, and (once) a Klevnaf war party that got lost. They trust no one who isn't family.
