A town near the Sondoros forest, existing primarily to support the wine industry. During harvest season, Piedritas swells with workers; the rest of the year, it maintains equipment, ages wine in vast cellars, and waits.

The town has developed its own culture, distinct from coastal Qintas—rougher, more practical, defined by the annual rhythm of the harvest. Vineyard workers from across the region gather here, sharing techniques and gossip. Some are human; the Amverela prefer to employ outsiders for the physical labor.
