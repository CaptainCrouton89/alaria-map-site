The northern city on the Myusha River, controlling river trade from the northern forests and serving as Iqes's connection to Gyosha and the waters beyond. Less grand than Mjiqa, more practical — a working city where ships are built, goods are processed, and the fishing fleet operates.

Zukisani has a reputation for relatively honest dealing. The ring-holders here are merchants and shipwrights, not aristocrats playing political games. Qindo and foreign-descended residents mix more easily; the inequality remains, but the contempt is muted.

The shipyards of Zukisani are famous across the Seacleft Coast. The city also serves as the primary port for trade with Mirko and Gyosha — connections that bring both commerce and complications.
