A vast bog in southern Blyss where the boundary between land and water dissolves entirely. The Swallowing Mire extends for miles in every direction—a maze of stagnant pools, floating vegetation mats, and stretches of ground that look solid until you step on them. The water is dark, acidic, and home to things that pull prey under.

The mire marks the southern edge of Draphilir's claimed territory. Beyond it lies the transition zone between Blyss and the central Beastlands, and eventually the approaches to Disaster Passage. Crossing the mire is possible with guides; without them, it's suicide.
