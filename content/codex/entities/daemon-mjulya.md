**Domains:** Good will, friendship, generous act.

**Era of ascension:** Lost Ages (~15,000 BSD). **Cohort: current.** Mjulya rose during a period of extended hardship when genuine acts of mutual aid became the foundation of surviving communities; the accumulated gratitude of thousands of small kindnesses became her prayer base.

**Worshipped by:** Communities that organize around mutual obligation — village councils, guild welfare systems, kin networks that care for their vulnerable. She has no single dedicated culture; her worship appears diffusely across many. (The worships edge is authored downstream on the worshipper, not here.)

Mjulya and Talressses were once close — allied daemons whose domains (good will and persuasion, respectively) had obvious overlap. The alliance broke when Talressses moved to steal Bylzar's followers through manipulation. Mjulya, recognizing the tactic for what it was, withdrew her followers from supporting Talressses entirely. Talressses's power collapsed in the aftermath, and he redirected his ambitions toward the Shailin halflings of Sestros.

Mjulya does not pursue Talressses; she simply unmakes what he builds. When his manufactured faith fades — as it will — her kind of worship tends to fill the space.
