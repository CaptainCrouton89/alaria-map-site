Population: ~250

A village along the Sova Chyda's middle reaches, within sight of the Winterwood's edge. Asgrael serves pilgrims traveling to the sacred forest and foresters who harvest timber from the woodland's outer edges. (Unlike Klevnaf's total prohibition on logging, Istora permits sustainable harvesting from Winterwood's periphery—a theological difference that both sides consider evidence of the other's corruption.)
