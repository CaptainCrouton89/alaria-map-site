Sand dragons are not dragons, though they do have breath weapons like dragons, thus their names. They are large, tarrasque like lizard creatures that can belch cones of high speed sand which completely obliterate structures, like a high powered sand blast. It will completely destroy armor

About 40 feet long, 4 legs, lots of spines, scaly skin, brown/gold/yellow.
