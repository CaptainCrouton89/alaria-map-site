The southeastern arm of the Gül Mountains, curving down toward the broader Kharvorn range. The Sorgül are the wildest of the three Gül ranges—less settled, more dangerous, and home to things the giants prefer not to discuss.

#### Geography

The Sorgül rise in jagged peaks, younger and sharper than their northern cousins. Deep glacial cirques bite into the mountainsides, some holding small lakes, others filled with permanent ice. The terrain is treacherous even by giant standards—loose scree, hidden crevasses, and sudden storms that sweep up from the lowlands.
