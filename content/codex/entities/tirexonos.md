The capital, such as it is. Tirexonos sprawls across a cluster of islands where the Jiji River splits into multiple channels, its buildings raised on stilts above the floodwaters. This is the seat of whatever passes for central government in Nashua—the Council of Fang and Scale, where clan leaders gather to coordinate the endless war.

The city's architecture is distinctive: curving walls that echo serpentine movement, buildings that seem to grow organically from the terrain, and a maze-like layout that would trap invaders in killing zones. The central island holds the Temple of the Hunt, where the greatest Ix'Vasyla warriors are honored and where prisoners of particular importance are... processed.

Foreign visitors are rare but not unknown. Tirexonos maintains a small quarter for diplomats and merchants, heavily watched and easily isolated. The message is clear: you are here on sufferance.
