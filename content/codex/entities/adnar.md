The grain port at the south of Erasnus, positioned for trade with Ubrik and the southeastern routes. The silvertongue plague has made it the first harbor for the dwarves fleeing the dying north, and its warehouses now hold more people than grain — so when a season's grain stood surety by a Myornic factor failed to arrive, the shortfall fell on the town that could least absorb it, and the bond behind it became the charter case now stalled before the Alliance court at Aldriktch.
