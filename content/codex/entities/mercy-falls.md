Not a river but a spectacular waterfall where the Suftos Hills drop toward the coast. Water from the highland streams plunges several hundred feet into a mist-shrouded pool. The name is bitterly ironic—during the early years of the elven civil war, prisoners were thrown from the falls rather than fed through winter.
