A dwarven state in the southern Kharvorn Mountains, sharing a southeastern border with the state of Myorna. Hephake is known for its extensive silver mines and the pollution they produce.

#### The Pact of Barrik

The Pact of Barrik was a diplomatic tradition shared between Hephake and Argysis: a statue of two dwarven kings—one holding a battleax, one a book—placed at the border to represent both peoples. Princes from both kingdoms would spend days in meditation near it; the statue was said to impart wisdom. When Rothogomos conquered Argysis two centuries ago, the tradition died—the border is no longer safe to approach—and the statue now stands in territory Hephake cannot reach.

#### The Mountain Churches

Three ancient churches stand in the high passes of Hephake, built in the early days of the kingdom to sanctify the mountain roads and protect travelers. Each is dedicated to a different aspect of dwarven faith, and each has developed its own character over the centuries.
