A southern city near the Skyloft Castle ruins, drawing scholars and treasure-hunters interested in whatever caused the fortress to fall. Belén's economy has become dependent on these visitors—guides, supplies, lodging, and discreet medical care for those who return injured.

The city council officially discourages exploration of the ruins. The city economy would collapse without it.
