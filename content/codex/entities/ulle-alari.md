A settlement on the western edge of the plateau where it meets the Mathavia Mountains. Ulle Alari survived the giant invasion—barely—by virtue of its position in terrain too rough for the giants to bother with. The settlement is now home to ogre refugees, human scavengers, and others who make their living picking over the plateau's ruins. It's a rough, lawless place where questions about the past aren't welcome.
