A structure of impossible geometry hovering above the waves, home to a being called the Seahorse King—supposedly a god from another plane of existence. The Palace is the only place this entity can survive in Alaria; something about the intersection of sea, sky, and otherworldly geometry keeps it anchored to reality.

The Seahorse King grants audiences to those who reach the Palace, offering cryptic prophecies and occasionally material aid. What it wants in return is unclear. Visitors report that the interior of the Palace exists in more dimensions than the exterior suggests.
