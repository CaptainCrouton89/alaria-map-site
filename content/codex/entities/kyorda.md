Kyorda occupies the narrow coastal shelf where the Frostmarch Peninsula tapers toward the Denrik Sea, facing Wavecrash Sound to the southeast. It is the most settled of the peninsula's states—home to Tikhaya humans and a community of Wytrolape, giant apes who have claimed the sea-cliffs as their own. The two peoples share this rocky shore in an arrangement that has proved durable if not always easy, with the Tikhaya centered on the town of Xandur while the Wytrolape range the headlands above.

What sets Kyorda apart from the peninsula's other states is the Bank of Infindior, a financial institution of unusual reach for this latitude. Merchants and factors from farther south use the Bank as a waypoint for extending credit into the cold north, making Kyorda a modest hub for commerce that would otherwise bypass the peninsula entirely. The coin that flows through Infindior funds Tikhaya trading expeditions and, less directly, keeps the Wytrolape's cliff-side from becoming contested ground.
