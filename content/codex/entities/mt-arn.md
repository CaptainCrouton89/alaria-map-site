The southern mountain, lower and warmer than its twin. Rich in iron and copper, Mt. Arn attracted human miners centuries ago—they stayed, built a city, and eventually discovered they had neighbors.

**Vastilin** sprawls across the southern slopes: markets, foundries, warehouses, and all the comfortable chaos of human habitation. The mountain's wealth flows through here—both the metals mined from the rock and the services sold from Eynmore above.

**Features:**
- **Vastilin** (capital): Merchant city of humans, population ~12,000
- **The Iron Foundries**: Metal processing and smithing
- **The Great Market**: Trade hub for the western mountains
- **The Lower Tunnels**: Mining operations extending deep into the mountain
