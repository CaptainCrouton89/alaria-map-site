At the forest's center, on the banks of the Ver Ta'Eres, stands the ruins of Kilren's Palace. Built 3,000 years ago by King Kilren in defiance of the treaty. Now a monument to what happens when elves forget their place.

See the Kilren's Palace entry for the full story. In brief: ten thousand soldiers vanished overnight. The king returned mute and hollow. The finest titan-bone armory ever assembled sits untouched in the fortress, and will remain so forever, because no one who enters comes back unchanged.
