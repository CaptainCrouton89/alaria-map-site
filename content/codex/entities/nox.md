The easternmost Zelidian village, closest to the coast and to outsiders. Nox serves as the reluctant interface between Zelidia and the outside world—a shadow-market where non-pixies can trade under heavy restriction. Transactions occur at night (hence the name), conducted through intermediaries, with the actual Zelle traders remaining unseen in the darkness above. Weapons are forbidden. Lights are forbidden. Those who break the rules simply vanish.
