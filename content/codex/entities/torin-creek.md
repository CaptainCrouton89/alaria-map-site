A major tributary entering the Jiji from the west, Torin Creek provides access to the deep marsh territories. The upper reaches are navigable only by small craft and only by those who know the channels. This is where the most toxic, most dangerous, most Ix'Vasyla-controlled territory lies.

Invaders who push up Torin Creek don't come back. The marshes simply swallow them.
