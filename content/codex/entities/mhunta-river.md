A southern river flowing through Bobeitio and the agricultural lowlands before reaching the coast. Warmer and slower than the Tyura, the Mhunta supports the irrigation networks that make southern Poum Titch the nation's breadbasket.

Loyalist forces patrol the Mhunta heavily. It's their supply line, their lifeline, the one river they cannot afford to lose. So far the rebels haven't pushed this far south—but if Goipin falls back into loyalist hands, the Mhunta will be the next target.
