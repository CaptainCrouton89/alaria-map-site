The lake still exists, now surrounded by ruins rather than palatial estates. Drasnian refugees have established camps along its shores, using the water access as a foundation for their attempts at rebuilding something functional. The lake's fish provide food; its position provides defensibility. If any organized settlement emerges from Syvlius's chaos, it will probably center here.
