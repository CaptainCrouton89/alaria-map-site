The Blueshale Peaks stand on one of the deepest Golus seams in the northern reach, where earth-leyline pressure surfaces through pale stratified stone and the bedding runs thick and continuous through the range. Attunement to Golus runs at a depth here that the same bloodlines do not find in the lowlands. The Korrun arose in and beneath these peaks, and hold them still.
