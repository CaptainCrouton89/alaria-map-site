A dwarven city-state on the southern coast of SW Urok, famous for its School of Music and Theater. Maun sits where the Maun River empties into Beauty Bay, its harbor busy with ships carrying students, graduates, and the instruments they craft. See the full state entry for details on its martial traditions, mandatory service requirements, and the mysterious Ghost who haunts its halls.

The city is as much heard as seen—music drifts from every quarter, students practicing at all hours, the sounds of a hundred instruments blending into a constant, ever-shifting symphony. Visitors either fall in love with Maun or flee within a day. There is no middle ground.
