The easternmost river, closest to the Dalizi border and the School of Summoning. The Riki Valley is the most rural and the most Rentar-influenced—fewer human farms, more monasteries, and a growing atmosphere of religious intensity.

**Major Settlements:**
- **Prozny** (~5,000): The largest town in the Riki Valley, serving as a waystation for travelers to the School of Summoning.
