A holding on the western edge of Adron, emptied at the Megélren Exile and left to rot. It was a waystation of the Waterdark order's shadow-arm, abandoned when that faction was struck from the house-rolls and put out of the kingdom. The killing-cells that descend from Serileni's exiles still know the road to it. None of them keep it.
