The Shin-keth are gnolls born into shadow. They run far larger than common gnolls and far stronger, and a grown one can step into a patch of darkness and simply not be there anymore. The trait breeds true. It is a lineage, not a working laid on a single animal, and a Shin-keth whelp comes into the world already half in the dark its parents hunt from.

What they carry is a Vulus affinity, an inborn pull toward the darkness layer that lets them thicken the shadow around them and pour themselves into it. That is shadow worked as a substance, the same stuff a dark-shaper pools and shapes, not a hole cut in the light and nothing to do with the dead. The gift sits over an ordinary living beast. A Shin-keth eats, breeds, and ages, and when one is killed it dies the plain death of any animal: the three strands take their roads, soul to the Astral and shadow to Malstaris and spirit to Celestia while its name is still spoken, and the shadow-gift ends with the body that carried it.
