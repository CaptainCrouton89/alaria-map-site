Deep, purple color. Dragon born under the Killing Moon (a.k.a. the Third Moon). Possesses all but one of the elemental stones of darkness—has them inset in his forehead, and many of the stones of void as well. 

Hunts the elves of the Twilight Forest, as well as the beasts there. 

Lair is located in void-locked location, shrouded in absolute darkness (even darker than rest of forest). Built in caves in some random rock formations.
