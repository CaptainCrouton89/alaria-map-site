A trading post and waystation on the eastern edge of Kura, where the dunes begin to give way to the rocky terrain approaching the Wyrm Teeth. Fata M'darah marks the traditional boundary between Kura's settled territory and the wilder lands beyond.

The waystation is built around a natural rock formation that provides shade and shelter. A small permanent population maintains the wells and guesthouses, but most of those present at any time are travelers—merchants heading east toward the Mirrored Palace, scholars bound for the Wyrm Teeth ruins, or the occasional fool seeking fortune in the deep desert.

The name means "the daughter's rest" in old Kuran, though who the daughter was and why she rested here is lost to time.
