The Glivornaxi are a people of the orc, not a separate biological lineage — swamp-guardians whose identity is built on the guardian role and deep attunement to Plenjorn's rhythms, not on any biological split from orcish kind. The Glivornaxi have dark eyes and muddy green skin, living in harmony with the natural world around the Grand Tolkarsus as it passes through the Plenjorn Swamp. This mystical swamp has regenerative properties that heal those who bathe in its waters, and the Glivornaxi have become its guardians.

They are skilled spear-fighters, using their weapons primarily as defense against the Shazuihni boar riders who frequently raid their territory. Their connection to nature runs deep, and they understand the swamp's moods and secrets better than any outsider ever could.
