Tybalexyn was a gnome cartographer of the Great Expansion, born somewhere in the Pelican Isles diaspora, who spent sixty years mapping skystone trade routes and high-altitude navigation points. Her charts are the foundational document for any sky-route claim, and Shoryaven borrows from them without attribution — a slight she documented in a footnote that outlived his goodwill.
