**Domains:** Health, strength.

**Era of ascension:** Great Expansion (~4,000 BSD). **Cohort: current.** Qoponatai emerged as expanding populations strained healing resources and warrior cultures proliferated; the combination of health and strength in a single deity spoke to a pragmatic theology — the body as both instrument and temple.

**Worshipped by:** Healers who also train to defend their patients, warrior traditions that invest in keeping their fighters alive, and cultures where physical capability is a survival necessity rather than a prestige marker. (The worships edge is authored downstream on the worshipper, not here.)

The apparent contradiction in Qoponatai's domains — health (preservation) and strength (force) — reflects the daemon's core theology: that the two are not opposites but prerequisites. His following resists the more comfortable deities that offer one without the other.
