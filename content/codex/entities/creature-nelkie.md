Nelkie are small, fae-born creatures that spawn in areas of intense sadness or despair.They feed on teh misery, spreead it, and will spoil food, destroy delicate objects, and generally make everything worse. Will flee if approached. Will disappear when sadness gone. They are the counterpart to sprites.
