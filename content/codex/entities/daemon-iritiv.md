**Domains:** Theft, attention, alacrity.

**Era of ascension:** Great Expansion (~2,000 BSD). **Cohort: current.** Íritiv rose as trade cities grew and urban density created both opportunity and competition; her domain feeds on the attention economy of crowded places.

**Worshipped by:** Thieves' guilds, scouts, acrobats, pickpockets, and those who make their living through speed and misdirection. She has no temples — her shrines are small, temporary, and hidden. (The worships edge is authored downstream on the worshipper, not here.)

Íritiv's domain of *attention* is the subtler half of her power: her followers are exceptionally skilled at reading a room, tracking a target's gaze, and performing under pressure. What looks like simple theft is, to her devotees, a practice of perfect presence.
