Deeper in the forest, Hickton serves the hunting and trapping trade. Furs, hides, and forest products flow through here. The town maintains better relations with the Yngli communities than most Mueran settlements, partly through necessity—Hickton cannot survive without forest access, and the Yngli control that access.
