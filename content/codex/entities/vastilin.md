The human capital on Mt. Arn. A city of commerce, craft, and comfortable excess.

Vastilin is everything Eynmore isn't—loud, crowded, messy, alive. Markets compete for attention. Foundries pour smoke. Taverns overflow. It's a proper city, even if it's built on a mountainside in the middle of nowhere.

**Architecture**: Practical construction in local stone, expanded piecemeal over centuries. The city has no plan—buildings were added where needed, creating a maze of streets that locals navigate by instinct.

**Government**: The **Merchant Council** of seven major families controls city affairs. The **First Merchant**, currently **Aldous Brennan**, mediates between them and represents Vastilin in joint decisions with Eynmore.

**Character**: Busy. Pragmatic. Focused on the next deal, the next shipment, the next opportunity. Visitors find it refreshingly normal after the strangeness of Rhea culture.
