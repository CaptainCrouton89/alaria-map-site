Istora's lifeline. This clear river flows from springs in the Suftos Hills through the Winterwood and past Tuur to the sea. The Istori have built their civilization along its banks—it provides drinking water, irrigation, and transport. Unlike Murder Creek, its waters are pure. The elves say this proves their faction's righteousness.
