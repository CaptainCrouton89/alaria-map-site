Argysis is a sub-range of the Kharvorn Mountains that once housed one of the great dwarven kingdoms. It has been conquered by the black dragon Rothogomos, who now rules from the ruined capital of Othyndria. The population—mostly Shyka dwarves—have been brainwashed over generations to worship their conqueror as a savior.

#### The Dragon's Domain

Rothogomos seized Argysis roughly two centuries ago, betraying his brother Tepheranos (who now rules Anarak to the south) to claim both the kingdom and its treasury. The dragon has transformed what was once a prosperous dwarven civilization into a militarized cult dedicated to his glory and focused on eventual revenge against his brother.

The surface of Argysis is dotted with abandoned holds, collapsed tunnels, and the ruins of what was once a thriving civilization. The dwarves who remain live in the functional portions of their ancestors' works, emerging only to serve Rothogomos's will.

#### Pact of Barrik

The Pact of Barrik is a statue of two dwarven kings, standing back to back, one holding a battleax, the other holding a book. These corresponded to the Hephakean and Argysisean kings respectively.

The statues are said to imbue wisdom in those who spend time aroudn them. Children from both kingdoms would visit the statue, and princes would spend days in meditation around them. When Argysis was conquered by Rothogomos, this was no longer safe, and the tradition died out.
