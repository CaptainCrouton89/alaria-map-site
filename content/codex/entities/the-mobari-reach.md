The eastern portion of the Dunes, where sand gives way to rocky hardpan approaching Shyona's borders, is called the **Mobari Reach**. The terrain here is more stable but no less harsh—flat, sun-baked earth broken by occasional rock outcrops and seasonal creek beds that flow only during rare storms.

The Mobari Reach serves as the main overland approach to Gnotobi from Shyona. A poorly maintained road connects the lake to Shyona's western settlements, used primarily by those who can't afford river transport or need to avoid Orangeport's scrutiny.
