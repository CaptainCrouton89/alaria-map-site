The seat of the Grand Imperial Ecclesiarch sits where the Honor River bends through the heart of Avalon. Tarolin is both capital and pilgrimage site, its central temple-palace housing the throne where the Ecclesiarch has sat, unblinking, for longer than anyone can remember.

The city is organized in concentric rings around the temple. The innermost ring houses the Solum Impervium—the Ecclesiarch's administrative hand—and the highest priests. Each successive ring holds lesser religious officials, then merchants and craftsmen, then the common faithful. Movement between rings requires permission from the appropriate authorities, who consult the Ecclesiarch's pronouncements to determine who may pass.

Tarolin's population approaches 40,000, making it Ve's largest city outside Shyona. Pilgrims swell this number during holy seasons, when the faithful come to hear the Ecclesiarch's pronouncements about the coming year.
