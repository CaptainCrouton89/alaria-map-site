A range of forested hills in northwestern Qet Yemani, marking the transition between the jungle lowlands and the western highlands. The hills are less densely vegetated than the deep jungle, with rocky outcrops and small clearings. They serve as a natural defensive barrier and contain several minor passes leading west.
