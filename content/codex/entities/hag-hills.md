Foothills of the Dragon's Spine in the northeast, marking the transition from Crimson Coast to Dragon's Spine Coast. Named for the old women who supposedly live there, dispensing prophecy and curses in equal measure. Whether these hags are real, metaphorical, or something else entirely depends on who you ask.
