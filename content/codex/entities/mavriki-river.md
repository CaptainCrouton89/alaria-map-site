An eastern river running through the hills toward Whitefin Bay. Less vital than the Ovuluria but still contested—whoever controls the Mavriki controls access to the eastern coast and the towns that depend on it.

The fighting here has been sporadic, more raids than battles. Both sides probe each other's positions, testing for weakness. The villages along the Mavriki have learned to hide when soldiers approach, regardless of which banner they fly.
