The titans were created by Azus. They were the first beings on Alaria. The first titans were also gods—Nydus, Aurus, and Lyzaria. 

The titans had Azus's magic in their bodies, providing them strength. They had no soul or shadow. They lacked emotion since they were created only in Azus's image. It wasn't until the Ezz Rift that any creatures of Alaria could *feel*.
