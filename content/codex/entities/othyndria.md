The former capital, now Rothogomos's lair. Othyndria was built around a natural spring—the source of Wisdom Creek—and its great halls once housed the treasures and archives of the Argysisean kings. Now it is a dragon's hoard, jealously guarded and rarely seen by any living creature except those summoned to serve.

The city's upper levels are destroyed, collapsed by dragon-fire during the conquest. The lower levels remain intact, filled with Rothogomos's accumulated wealth and whatever dark things have taken root in the centuries since.
