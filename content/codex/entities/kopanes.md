A mining settlement in the western valleys, where Breian prospectors extract copper and tin from the mountain rock. The mines here are ancient—some shafts date back to whoever built Shtaneres—and occasionally break through into older workings with their own dangers. Kopanes is known for practical, hard people who don't ask too many questions about what their ancestors left behind.
