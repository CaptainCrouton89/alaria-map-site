Grømdrak was a dwarven genealogist of the Gondurak deep-clans, writing in the early Seventh Dawn. He is the authority on Norse-branch dwarf succession disputes and clan-boundary claims, and the only scholar to have traced the Strømgodden lineage back to pre-Long-Winter oral records. Erindath cites him twice; both times with visible reluctance.
