High in the Mountains Semaphois stands the wind-racked, abandoned Temple of the Wind. This remote structure was once home to monks and primeshapers who mastered both earth and air magic, using the mountain winds to fuel their practice. The temple was destroyed when a mechwurm attacked, killing the monks and leaving the temple empty.

Now the temple stands as a haunting reminder, its halls filled with the howling winds that once served the monks' magic. The structure itself remains intact, though battered by decades of relentless wind, and the knowledge the monks possessed has been lost with their deaths.
