The Rhea capital on Mt. Disyus. A city of contemplation, scholarship, and mastery.

Eynmore doesn't feel like a city to human visitors—it's too quiet, too orderly, too empty of the bustle that defines urban life. The Rhea don't crowd together; they space themselves for focus. The result is a settlement that seems half-populated even when everyone is present.

**Architecture**: Carved from mountain stone, elegant but austere. Buildings are integrated into the rock rather than imposed upon it. Open spaces allow air to flow—the Rhea need to feel the wind.

**Government**: The **High Contemplative** leads, currently **Whisper-of-Still-Waters**—a Rhea so old and focused that she barely acknowledges the physical world. She speaks rarely. When she does, Eynmore listens.

**Character**: Quiet. Patient. Dedicated to pursuits measured in decades. Visitors find it peaceful or maddening depending on temperament.
