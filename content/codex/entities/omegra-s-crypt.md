Omegra's Crypt is an Eloweir tomb in the southern hills, a short way north of the Glass Forest. It was cut long before Old Tolaria fell, when this was quiet border country and not a watched one. The Eloweir still tend it, which is how the older dead here outlast the towers that came centuries later.
