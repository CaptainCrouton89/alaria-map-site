The northwestern peninsula, a finger of jungle extending toward the Sea of Seven Snakes. Named for the serpents that infest its waterways—some venomous, some constrictors large enough to take a man, and rumors of something larger still in the deep interior.

Vassum nominally controls Jungula Serpentine, but "control" means they patrol the coast and maintain a few logging camps. The interior belongs to the snakes and whatever else lives there. Pirates from the Sea of Seven Snakes sometimes use the peninsula's hidden coves as anchorages, and Vassum's navy—such as it is—plays an eternal game of cat and mouse with smugglers running contraband through the serpent-infested waters.
