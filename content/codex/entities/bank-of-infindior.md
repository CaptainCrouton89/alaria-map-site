This unassuming counting-house on the Kyorda shore is the only way in or out of the Bank of Infindior, the pocket where time does not pass. The door is here; the vault is not, exactly — it sits outside time and is reached only through this threshold. The Adron houses that use it most are a short sail east along the Denrik Sea. The plane entry carries what the bank is and why a deposit there is as much a weapon as a safekeeping.
