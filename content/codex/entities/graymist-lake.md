Large lake in the southern forest, source of the fog that gives Graymist Forest its name. Beautiful and eerie. The water is dark, the shores muddy, the silence absolute.

Things live in Graymist Lake. The local Gyv know better than to fish certain depths or swim at certain times. They've made arrangements. Outsiders who ignore warnings occasionally vanish.
