Qet Yemani is the largest of the five jungle nations, controlling the western reaches of the Jungles of Godahi. Its vast territory encompasses the great Tora Mazîl forest, extensive swamplands, and the western highlands where the jungle meets the mountains. Where other jungle nations measure their domains in tens of miles, Qet Yemani spans over 120 miles from its eastern borders to the Hekat Valley in the west.

#### Geography

Qet Yemani's territory is defined by three major terrain types: the elevated forest of Tora Mazîl, the treacherous swamplands scattered throughout, and the western valleys and hills.

- **North**: Borders Da Trang, with Darkwater Swamp and the Greenflow river marking the frontier
- **East**: Jungle frontiers with New Chimea and Vystrilik, with Deadwood Swamp in the eastern interior
- **South**: Borders Eoga, where the Misty Marsh begins
- **West**: The Hills of Anaotki and Hekat Valley mark the transition to the western highlands

The **Hekat River** flows south through the western portion of Qet Yemani, draining the Hekat Valley. The **Greenflow** runs along the northeastern border with Da Trang.
