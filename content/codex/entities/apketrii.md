The mountainous island home to Keletus. The Diamondback Mountains run through its center, the Hallenfell range guards the northwest, and Blossom Valley provides the agricultural south. The southeastern peninsula—called the Long Reach—stretches toward Ve, its lighthouse towns watching the sea lanes.

The island's most mysterious feature is Hallenfell, where drowned ruins of a pre-minotaur civilization—the Kelemyr—emerge from cliff faces. Expeditions into the flooded halls rarely return.
