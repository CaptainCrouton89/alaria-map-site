Ayashk Island is a small, rocky archipelago rising from the Deaddore Strait. The main island is dominated by pale-gold hills of scrub and loose stone, steep enough to discourage farming but well suited to the small fortified harbors the Colonies rely on. Several smaller outcroppings cluster to the west, most too exposed for permanent habitation; the sheltered anchorages between them are the island's most valuable geographic feature.

The three settlements of the Free Colonies occupy the main island's central ridge and its eastern slopes. Drusikia sits on the northern side, its harbor facing the strait's western approach. Ashk, the largest settlement and the confederation's assembly seat, occupies the central high ground where the ridge levels into a defensible plateau. Vania lies close to Ashk on the eastern slope, its quay aimed toward the open Riptear Sea. The tight spacing of all three reflects the island's limited flat land rather than any planned proximity — each grew up where the terrain allowed.

The island's interior offers little beyond firewood and grazing. Freshwater comes from cisterns and a handful of seasonal springs along the ridge. Most food is traded in; the island's economy is maritime to its core. For the confederation that governs the island, see the Free Colonies.
