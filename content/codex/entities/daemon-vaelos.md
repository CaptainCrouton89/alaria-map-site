**Domains:** War, honor, conquest.

**Era of ascension:** Golden Age of Man (~160,000 years ago). **Cohort: dead.** **Died:** God War & Long Winter (~75,000 years ago), killed by the very conflict his theology glorified.

**Worshipped by:** The warrior aristocracies of the Golden Age civilizations — kings' armies, professional soldier classes, those for whom war was a sacred vocation. No living culture maintains his worship. (The worships edge is authored downstream on the worshipper, not here.)

Vaelos did not cause the God War — the God War was the product of every major deity's followers competing for resources and dominance simultaneously — but his theology of *honorable conquest* gave the warring factions their vocabulary. Every army in the God War claimed to be fighting in a way Vaelos would sanction. The god himself died with all the others when Dyos's follower triggered the Kajiit eruption; whether he found the end honorable is not recorded.
