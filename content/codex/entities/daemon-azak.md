**Domains:** Time, clairvoyance, omens.

**Era of ascension:** Lost Ages (~20,000 BSD). **Cohort: current.** Azak rose during the long darkness between Craggus's death and the current era, sustained by the persistent human need to know what comes next.

**Worshipped by:** Seers, diviners, augurs, and travelers who interpret signs before a journey. His following has no single cultural home; every culture that practices divination contributes to his prayer pool. (The worships edge is authored downstream on the worshipper, not here.)

Ancient enough that his name appears in scholarly texts from before the Great Expansion, Azak has never commanded a large following — but an unbroken one. He requires neither temples nor priests; a traveler reading smoke is enough.
