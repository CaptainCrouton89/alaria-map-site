The northernmost lake, where the Krelfassa river meets the coast. D'had is the shallowest of the three and the stillwater effect is relatively mild—boats can cross with effort. The river mouth creates a mixing zone where normal water meets stillwater, producing strange currents that curl back on themselves.

Local legend claims D'had was once a harbor in ancient times, before the leylines shifted. No archaeological evidence supports this, though the lakebed is littered with debris too deep to recover easily.
