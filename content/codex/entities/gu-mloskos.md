The northwestern peninsula of Konai Mora, jutting into the Ocean of Azuros north of Solhalven. Gümloskos is mountainous and wind-scoured, its peaks lower than the Blistering Mountains to the south but no less treacherous. This is the domain of Zosuun, the albino ice-fire dragon who has claimed the region since before recorded history.

The Valkyries maintain a careful neutrality with Zosuun. The dragon's hunting grounds overlap with their patrol routes, but neither party seems interested in conflict. Travelers seeking passage through Gümloskos are advised to move quickly and avoid camping in the open—Zosuun has been known to steal memories from those who linger in his territory.
