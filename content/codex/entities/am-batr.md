A smaller port town on the bay's northern shore, Am Batr handles the fishing trade and serves as a waypoint for coastal traffic moving between the Tarns and the main Mueran ports. The town maintains a small shipyard that repairs rather than builds—good enough for fishing boats and coastal traders.
