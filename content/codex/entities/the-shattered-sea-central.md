A large ocean with thousands of islands dotting its entire area. The heart of the Western Isles is this chaotic archipelago—hundreds of distinct islands, atolls, and shoals packed into treacherous waters. Navigation here requires expert knowledge; the maze of islands has claimed countless ships.
