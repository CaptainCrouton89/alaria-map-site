A smaller lake in the central region, near the town of Opela Sopona. Unlike its larger cousin, Sopona Strempele welcomes visitors—fishing boats work its waters, and swimming is a popular summer activity.

Local legend claims the two Sopona lakes were once connected, separated when a sun elf priest raised the land between them to create privacy for Enuau Kemjallb. The geological evidence is ambiguous.
