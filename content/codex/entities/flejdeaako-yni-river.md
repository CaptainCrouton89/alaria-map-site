The primary river feeding Sopona Fia, flowing from the Torulius Mountains through the northern lowlands. The Kryaaji boundary markets operate along this river—the closest most traders ever get to the sun elf city.

The river's name is Kryaaji, roughly translating to "golden approach." In certain light, the water does seem to carry a golden tint, though whether this is mineral content, leyline influence, or elven magic is debated.
