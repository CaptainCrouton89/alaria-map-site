The only significant settlement in Kirthdron Soytas. Sarki Tulo was a support village for harvesting operations in the deeper mountains. It sits abandoned now, like all the southern sites—intact, empty, waiting.

Some expedition proposals have focused on Sarki Tulo specifically, arguing that its position at the edge of the contaminated zone might make it safer to explore. The priesthood has rejected all such proposals. The contamination doesn't respect convenient boundaries.
