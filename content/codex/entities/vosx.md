The second city, positioned where the Jiji River approaches the coast. Vosx handles most of Nashua's limited foreign trade—the exports of marsh-harvested alchemical ingredients, rare venoms, and the occasional ransom negotiation for captured enemies.

Vosx is also the center of the **Arcane Consortium of Nashua**, a scholarly institution that has somehow maintained academic pursuits despite the constant warfare. The Consortium studies the marshes' unique properties, develops new applications for local toxins, and maintains the only significant library in the region. They once placed a 50,000 gold bounty on information about Yegnoth—a planar anomaly in Gorathi territory—that remains unclaimed after two hundred years.
