The main road connecting Enymu to Tarkhetan and the rest of Tarkhon. Well-maintained by Enymese standards (which means passable but not paved), the Greenway carries most of the grain, wool, and livestock that flows from Enymu to Nektuna's markets.

Traveling the Greenway, you pass through the heart of Enymu's farmland: villages every few miles, fields stretching to the horizon, the occasional fortified manor house where local lords administer their lands.
