A formation of standing stones on a prominent hilltop in eastern Phriorys, their origin unknown. The stones predate Ix'Meglyakuk presence in the region—possibly predate intelligent life on Ve entirely. They're arranged in a pattern that doesn't match any known astronomical alignment, their surfaces carved with symbols no one can read.

The Ix'Meglyakuk use the Gathering Stones for inter-tribal councils, treating the site as neutral ground. Even Pyaganos respects the location; he's never hunted there, never interrupted a gathering. Some believe the stones hold power that constrains even dragons. Others believe the dragon simply finds the meetings useful—a way to observe tribal politics without direct involvement.
