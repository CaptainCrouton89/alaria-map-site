A ruined fortress on the Ovuluria River, strategically positioned between Tadinu and the eastern territories. The fortress dates to an earlier age—some say pre-Scalawag, built by whatever people held this land before the goblins came.

Both sides want Tuo. Its walls still stand, its position commands river traffic, and whoever holds it controls the Ovuluria's middle reaches. Skirmishes have been fought in its shadow. So far, neither army has committed to a full siege. The ruins are said to be haunted, which gives even battle-hardened Scalawags pause.
