A mysterious structure in the southwestern mountains, near Eyrar. Enki's House appears to be a single building of unusual architecture—curved walls, no visible doors at ground level, and windows placed too high for human use. Local legend holds it was built by a giant or titan named Enki who retreated here in ancient times.

The house is larger inside than outside. Explorers who have entered through the high windows report corridors that loop back on themselves, rooms that appear in different configurations on subsequent visits, and the persistent sense of being watched. Some claim to have heard breathing from behind the walls.

Whatever Enki was, if Enki ever existed, the house remains. Breians avoid it. The few treasure hunters who've gone in searching for giant artifacts have emerged empty-handed and shaken—or haven't emerged at all.
