The forest covering northern Moigos, stretching from the Qulyintes River to the coast near Blink Creek. Alegheim is dense, wet, and dark — the canopy blocks most sunlight, and the ground is perpetually soggy. The trees are adapted to standing water, their roots spreading wide and shallow.

The Kappa consider Alegheim their natural territory. The swampy forest suits their physiology, and they've maintained hidden places throughout — meeting spots, caches, bolt-holes for when affairs in town become complicated. Humans and other races live in Alegheim too, in villages along the rivers and clearings, but they live with the understanding that the forest belongs to others.

The forest's edge approaches the Isle of the Night and the Gloomterror's influence. The western reaches of Alegheim are darker than they should be, and the things that live there are stranger than ordinary swamp creatures.
