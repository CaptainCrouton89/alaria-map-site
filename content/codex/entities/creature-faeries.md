Faeries are beings of light. They are servants of Aurus. They primarily reside on the isles of the Astral Plane, but can be found on the Material Plane during a Faesummer. Though they serve a deity of light, faeries are fae-natured creatures who can hear Faesong — Melera's ambient song running through all living things — and channel it to work magic; their allegiance to Aurus does not diminish this sensitivity, which is native to their nature rather than granted by any deity.
