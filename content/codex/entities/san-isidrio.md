A central city along the main road between Cañas and Qintas. San Isidrio has positioned itself as a waystation for travelers and merchants, offering comfortable inns, reliable supplies, and absolutely nothing memorable. This is intentional—the city profits from traffic passing through, not from giving anyone reason to linger.
