Gorgers are a people of the giant, not a separate bloodline — nomadic consumption is their way of life, not a biological departure from giant stock.

Gorgers are nomadic scavengers, massive engines of consumption that roam the land eating everything in their path—entire herds of cattle, trees, and unlucky travelers. They are universally feared and reviled. In the north, they also range the glacial Giants' Corridor through Grustos, where the terrain funnels mammoth and aurochs migrations into narrow passes the Gorgers trail and strip bare.
