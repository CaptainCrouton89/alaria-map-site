Swampland where the forest meets standing water. Difficult terrain, unpleasant conditions, and fiercely independent communities who've chosen isolation over involvement in Gyv politics.

The Bittermarsh Gyv are considered strange by their own people—more direct, less concerned with elaborate manipulation. Some say the swamp breeds honesty because there's no one worth manipulating. Others say they're playing a longer game than anyone realizes.
