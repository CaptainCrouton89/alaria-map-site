The second city, traditionally controlled by one of the now-headless houses. Zunuki specialized in jewelry—combining Ohblex gem expertise with artistic design that fetched premium prices across the Middle Sea.

The city is in turmoil. House Wizzik has claimed control, but the local craftsmen are loyal to their dead masters. Production has slowed, quality has suffered, and everyone is waiting to see what happens next.
