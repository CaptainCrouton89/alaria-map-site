The Dark Ages span 0–231 SD, the opening chapter of the Seventh Dawn.

The Titansworn were Deoric cultists — believed to be related to the Vyanoweir — who sought to reawaken the titan Hykravones. They formed temples and research studies in the ruins of the titans and were everywhere across Alaria. They believed the titans could save the world after it burned, and they pursued that goal with fanatical devotion.

The Dark Ages ended with a war against the Titansworn, in which Athatgarun and Raiyurna were involved. Their defeat closed this opening chapter of the Seventh Dawn and allowed the civilizations of Alaria to begin building what would become the modern political world.
