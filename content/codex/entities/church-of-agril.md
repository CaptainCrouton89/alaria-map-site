The easternmost church, closest to the Myorna border, honors the stone-father aspect—endurance, patience, and the slow wisdom that comes from centuries of contemplation. The Church of Agril is quiet where the others are active, its priests devoted to prayer, meditation, and the maintenance of genealogical records stretching back millennia.

The church houses the most complete archive of Hephakean lineages outside the capital. Families send representatives to update their entries, verify ancestry, and record births and deaths. The priests are meticulous, impartial, and utterly disinterested in politics—which makes them invaluable arbiters in inheritance disputes.

The Church of Agril also watches the Myorna border with quiet concern. The Sivakr's memory magic troubles the stone-father priests deeply. They have begun maintaining duplicate records in secret locations, just in case.
