The open water east of Phyr Island, connecting to the Sea of Fools to the south. Further Sound is relatively safe—the curse's effects diminish rapidly east of the Statue Hills. Ships approaching Attla come this way, and the sound sees occasional traffic from traders willing to deal with the islanders.

The name reflects the sound's position: further from Ve's mainland than any other named water, at the edge of the known world before the poisoned beauty of the Sea of Fools begins.
