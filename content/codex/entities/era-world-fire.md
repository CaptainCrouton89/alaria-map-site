The World Fire is a catastrophic event occurring approximately 10 BSD — marked as zero on many calendars and serving as the boundary between the BSD (Before Seventh Dawn) and SD (Seventh Dawn) dating systems.

The World Fire stands as the civilizational threshold of the Modern Era. Its precise nature and cause remain contested by scholars; what is agreed is that it reset the calendar and defined the world that followed. The Seventh Dawn emerged from its ashes.
