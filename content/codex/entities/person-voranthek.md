Voranthek was a human mage-scholar of the invented-harsh desert cultures, writing late in the Great Expansion, who dedicated his career to mapping the force leyline network and documenting its physical effects on terrain, water, and settlements built across its paths. He is the primary authority for any claim about how leylines bleed into the material world — the still lakes, the motion-arrested zones, the places where kinetic force simply stops.
