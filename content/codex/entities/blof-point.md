The southernmost settlement in Mueras, a windswept village on the tip of the Beak. Ferry services to North Agreben depart from here when weather permits. The village exists in a state of perpetual salt-spray dampness; nothing stays dry, nothing stays rust-free, and the locals have developed a resigned acceptance of entropy.
