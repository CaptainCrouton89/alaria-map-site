A smaller tributary in the eastern forest, flowing south from the Kilbyurn foothills. The Cyatri marks the rough boundary between deep ulvsjael territory and the transition zone approaching Balduahr's patrols.

The river is fast and rocky, descending from the mountains through a series of rapids and small waterfalls. The noise of the water can mask approaching threats—or mask your own movement, depending on perspective. Ulvsjael packs hunt along the Cyatri less frequently than along the interior rivers, as the terrain suits their tactics less well.
