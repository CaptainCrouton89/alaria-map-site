The village closest to Glezamos, practically a suburb of the capital. Yift's artisans produce the material culture of Zelidian religion: ritual blades, wing-paints in sacred patterns, sacrificial implements, and the bone-chimes that hang throughout every Zelle settlement. Yift's workshops never fall silent.
