Shir is a hill-spined island set in open water midway between the northern mainland and the Sunshine Coast. The **Scream Strait** runs along its northern shore and the **Lightning Passage** along its south, the two channels leaving it without a land border to any neighbor. Its broken, ridged interior and surrounding shoals keep it apart from the larger landmasses on every side.
