Honygar, called hardstone across the western trades, is a dense grey rock that resists Kethic earth-shaping almost completely. An earth-worker who can fold common granite like wet clay finds honygar inert under the same effort. The leyline pressure slides off it and the stone does not move. Builders pay well for that. Vault doors and treasury walls are cut from honygar where the budget allows, and so are the foundations of anything a Kethic caster might otherwise reshape from the inside.
