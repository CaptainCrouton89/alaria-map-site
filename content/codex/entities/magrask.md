A mountain-fed river descending from the Dragon's Spine into northeastern Tangiern. Cold, fast, and prone to flash floods during snowmelt. The Magrask valley provides one of the few routes through the northern mountains, though "route" is generous—it's more a series of treacherous scrambles alongside waterfalls.
