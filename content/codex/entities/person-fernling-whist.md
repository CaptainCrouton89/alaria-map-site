Fernling Whist was a halfling hedge-recorder from a village south of Grendenheim who survived the World Fire (10 BSD) and spent the following forty years writing down everything she had seen and heard from other survivors. Her account is raw, untheoretical, and uniquely valuable for the World Fire's immediate ground-level effects on halfling communities — the one subject the grand chroniclers treat as a footnote.
