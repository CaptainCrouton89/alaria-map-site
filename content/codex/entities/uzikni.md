A mountain town at one of the few reliable passes through the Gemstone Peaks. Most trade enters Mkinti through Uzikni, which means the town has more contact with outsiders than most Ohblex settlements. The gnomes here are marginally more tolerant of non-gnomish company—emphasis on marginally.
