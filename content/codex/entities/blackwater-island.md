North of the main island, **Blackwater** takes its name from the dark, deep waters of its harbor. The island sits lower than its neighbors, with a more accessible coastline—though "accessible" is relative in Knova.

The fortress here is less imposing than the Iron Academy but more practical. Libraries, archives, supply warehouses—Blackwater stores knowledge rather than showcasing strength.

**Notable Features:**
- **The Archives**: Records of campaigns, supply calculations, and strategic analyses dating back centuries
- **The Charting Halls**: Where students learn terrain analysis and logistics planning
- **The Black Harbor**: Deep anchorage where supply ships dock, protected from storms
