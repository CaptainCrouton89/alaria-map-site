Besnoumeru is a wealthy merchant district within Tornia, known for traders who predict shifts in supply and demand with uncanny accuracy. Rents here are among the highest in the kingdom, and the district's position on the river delta makes it a crucial node in Tornia's broader trade network. The architecture reflects centuries of accumulated wealth—graceful warehouses and counting-houses lining the quays.

However, Besnoumeru's prosperity comes with a dark edge: the district is secretly controlled by time-gangs, criminal organizations that use temporal manipulation to maintain their iron grip on commerce and politics.
