The southwestern coast curves around Nuin Bay, a sheltered natural harbor that makes Mu possible. The bay's calm waters allow year-round shipping, connecting Sestros to the broader world despite its relative isolation on Ve.

Fishing villages dot the bay's shores, providing seafood to supplement the meat-heavy Sestran diet. The fishermen worship Talresses alongside everyone else, though their prayers focus on calm seas rather than fat flocks.
