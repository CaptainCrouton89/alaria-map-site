**Domains:** Flame, fire's destructive aspect.

**Era of ascension:** Lost Ages (~8,000 BSD). **Cohort: current.** Kiev and her twin Jingu ascended together — an unusual event in which two beings accreted faith simultaneously through a shared domain. Their joint ascension is the theological basis for why their worship requires honoring both equally.

**Worshipped by:** Fire mages, smiths, and warrior traditions that treat fire as a living force. Kiev and Jingu are worshipped as a pair; no culture worships one without the other. (The worships edge is authored downstream on the worshipper, not here.)

Kiev's aspect of flame is destructive — the fire that consumes and clears. Jingu governs flame's generative aspect. Together they hold the full domain. The pair possess extraordinary control over fire; individual miracles attributed to each twin are difficult to distinguish from the outside, but their devotees maintain the distinction is crucial.
