A town in the Sothills, serving as the center of Qintas's pastoral economy. Wool markets operate here year-round; cheese aging facilities line the main roads; shepherds from across the hills bring their flocks for sale and breeding.

The town's name—"radial hills"—refers to the way roads spread from its center like spokes on a wheel, connecting scattered hill estates to a central point. The Amverela planned it this way millennia ago. It still works.
