The eastern guardian of the Tarkhon Passage, watching the approaches from the Middle Sea.

Sharvatunis sits on Astrelle's eastern coast, positioned to intercept any fleet approaching from the east before it reaches the Needle. It's the first line of defense against naval threats from the Middle Sea kingdoms.

**Garrison**: ~300 soldiers, professional but not elite. Adequate equipment, adequate training. Good enough for a posting that rarely sees action.

**Posting**: Sharvatunis is neither prestigious nor punishing—it's simply a solid, respectable assignment. Officers serve their terms, do their jobs, and move on. Nothing exciting happens, but nothing terrible either.

**Commander**: **Captain Mira Solent**, a career officer in her forties. Competent, unambitious, and content to run a well-maintained fortress until retirement.
