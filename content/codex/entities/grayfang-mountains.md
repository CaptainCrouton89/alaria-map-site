Home to the Grayfang Goblins, a clan distinct from the Scalawags who use stone attunement to augment their teeth to enormous size. While many choose to enlarge their incisors into tusks, there's wide variation—some combine their front teeth into a single massive fang, others favor enlarged molars for crushing.

The Grayfangs have remained neutral in the civil war, watching from their mountain holds. Both sides have sent envoys seeking alliance. The Grayfangs have sent them back with polite refusals and not-so-polite warnings about what happens to armies that march through their territory.
