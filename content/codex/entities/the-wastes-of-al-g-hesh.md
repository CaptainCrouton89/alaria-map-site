The Wastes of Al G'hesh are a sun-scoured desert peninsula given over almost entirely to the dead. Where other lands bury their kings out of sight, Al G'hesh built outward: tomb-complexes, ossuary towns, and ceremonial courts spread across the dunes until the necropolis became the country. The living who remain keep the gates, the tombs, and the long rites owed to those interred here.
