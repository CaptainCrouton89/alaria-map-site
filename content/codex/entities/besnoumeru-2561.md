Nektuna's second city, built on a **time leyline**. The Oracle Merchants born here can sense market changes before they happen—not prophecy, but an instinct for timing that has made Besnoumeru almost as wealthy as Tarkhetan.

The city has an eerie calm. Nobody rushes. The merchants already know what's coming.
