The southern reaches of Pherezi Imnuttut are dominated by Giantfell—a mountainous region that earns its name. Giants dwell here, though what kind and how many remains unclear. Few explorers venture into Giantfell, and fewer return. Those who do speak of enormous shapes moving through the peaks, structures built for beings three times human height, and the constant threat of being stepped on by something that doesn't notice you.

The settlement of Themopolkrik clings to Giantfell's southern coast—a hardy fishing village that survives by staying beneath the giants' notice and fleeing to boats when that fails.
