A southwestern settlement near the edge of Breian territory, where the Drystone Mountains begin their descent toward the Giant Lands. Eyrar's people have the most contact with the giants and their vassals, trading mountain goods for lowland products. It's a rough town, accustomed to dealing with beings much larger than themselves.
