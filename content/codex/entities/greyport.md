Greyport is the southern port that sits nearest the Blades, and it pays for the distance. Chavux, the green dragon lairing in the islands, takes a share of the shipping bound in and out through the strait, and Greyport's hulls and crews are the ones most often on the water when he is hunting. The merchant houses here, and the larger ones at Shipmarsh up the coast, would pay well to have the dragon killed. But no Naga of Adron will raise a hand against a dragon, so any purse they raise has to find a foreigner willing to do it, and willing to risk whatever Adron does to a dragon-slayer afterward. None has collected yet.
